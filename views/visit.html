Getting Here

The Duquesne Incline is located just minutes from downtown Pittsburgh on the historic Mount Washington.

The lower station entrance is conveniently accessible from West Carson Street, where you'll find ample free parking in our dedicated lot. Exit at the designated Mount Washington ramp, follow West Carson Street, and look for clear signage directing you to the incline's entrance and parking area.
